A Better Future

IvyBrite is a world class university admissions consulting agency started by two Stanford alumni. Since 2016, we have operated under the philosophy of “real experiences, real outcomes.” Our team of professionals from MIT, Stanford, Harvard, Yale, Princeton, and UC Berkeley use their experience, knowledge of unspoken admission rules, and proven admission strategies to ensure that clients achieve their dream offers into prestigious universities around the world. Our consultants and editors are meticulously selected alumni from ivy league schools that have years of writing experience and application experience. With real experience, we achieve real outcomes. IvyBrite has helped hundreds of students gain admissions to their dream schools such as Harvard, Princeton, MIT, Stanford, Yale, and more. Your success is our success.
